About the Research

I. S. Shrair is the author of pioneering works on the Resonant Solar Cycle. His books explore the intricate relationships between solar activity, the planetary system, and terrestrial climate shifts. By bridging the gap between heliophysics and climate science, his work provides a new perspective on the cosmic influences governing our world.
