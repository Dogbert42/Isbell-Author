JOSHUA ISBELL

Author & Comedian

Joshua Isbell is an author and comedian based in Medford, Oregon.

Known for his "Self-Deprecating" style, he weaves together the sharp, observational wit of a stand-up comic with the profound narrative depth of a storyteller.

Whether on the page or on the stage.
