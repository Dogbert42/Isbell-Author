JOSHUA ISBELL

Author & Comedian

The Artist

Joshua Isbell is an author and comedian based in Medford, Oregon. Known for his "Midnight Editorial" style, he weaves together the sharp, observational wit of a stand-up comic with the profound narrative depth of a storyteller. Whether on the page or on the stage, Joshua explores the dualities of life—the happy, the unhappy, and the power influences in between.
